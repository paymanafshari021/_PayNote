**Description**

Modern  wireless networks must deliver high performance, strong security, and  operational simplicity at scale. In this technical workshop, we explore  how Fortinet’s WLAN portfolio integrates natively into the Security  Fabric, enabling secure wireless access, AI-driven operations, and  automated troubleshooting across the network.

Participants will gain hands-on experience with Fortinet Wireless LAN  solutions, including the latest platform capabilities, AI-powered  insights through AIOps, and integrated network security using the  Fortinet Security Fabric. The session focuses on practical operational  workflows such as deployment, monitoring, anomaly detection, and  automated troubleshooting in modern enterprise wireless environments.
