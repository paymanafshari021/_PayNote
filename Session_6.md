**Description**

This  session explores how organizations can deliver consistent Zero Trust  protection for every worker, regardless of where they connect from,  using the latest evolution of Universal ZTNA. Through hands-on labs,  participants will explore new capabilities and practical use cases that  demonstrate how modern ZTNA architectures reduce complexity while  strengthening security.
