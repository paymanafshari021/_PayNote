**Description**

This  session will open with an introduction to generative AI technologies  and trends, covering both public and locally hosted scenarios. We will  look at a range of threats and how recently introduced features in  FortiOS can be used to mitigate them.

The session includes a hands-on lab where participants will learn how  to identify and control access to public AI services, before looking at  challenges of locally hosted models, using a small CPU-based model,  where custom signatures will be developed to provide targeted  protection.
