**Description**

Cyber  threats don't wait — and neither should your SOC. This session explores  how FortiAnalyzer 8.0 transforms security operations from reactive log  management into a proactive, AI-driven defense platform. From automated  event handling to conversational AI investigation, this session shows  you how to put FortiAnalyzer's most powerful SOC capabilities to work  immediately.

Key Takeaways:

\- Event Handlers — Build smart, automated workflows that respond to  threats in real time, freeing your analysts to focus on what matters  most

\- VirusTotal Connector — Eliminate guesswork during triage by  instantly enriching alerts with real-world malware intelligence and  reputation data

\- FortiAI Agents: Alert Triage — Harness AI to automatically assess  and prioritize incoming alerts, dramatically reducing mean time to  respond

\- FortiAI Agents: Vulnerability Investigation — Empower your team to  stay ahead of attackers by using AI to continuously surface and  investigate vulnerable assets

\- FortiAI Chatbot — Ask your SOC anything — the FortiAI Chatbot lets  analysts investigate malicious activity across the network through  simple, natural language conversations Leave this session with hands-on  experience across FortiAnalyzer's most impactful SOC features — ready to  move from reactive monitoring to intelligent, AI-assisted security  operations.
